Settings:

Select your preferred restrooms

In other words, which ones are you closest to?

This setting determines which numbers show up first in the app icon. For example, if you are closest to the South, restrooms 1 and 2 will appear if they are open. You'll only see 3 or 4 if 1 and 2 are both occupied. Vice versa for the North. Changing this setting will not take effect until the next time a restroom opens or closes.
